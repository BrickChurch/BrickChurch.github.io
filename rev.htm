FIRESTICK APPS

Below are brief descriptions of some firestick apps. (Listed alphabetically). If the Rev has programmed your firestick many of these are already installed. This is to help you understand what some of them do to get you started. You may not use them all, but they are available.

CyberFlix, LiveNet TV, and Tubi are three main apps that should satisfy all you TV needs. These apps basically give you everything that you get on Cable TV, Netflix, Hulu, Amazon Prime, and more, for free! All you need is a good internet connection.

Xfinity and Players Klub require paid accounts.
* If and when using Xfinity, whatever you have on your Comcast / Xfinity account (HBO, etc.) will be available in this app. Vdeo On Demands (VODs) accessed on the Xfinity app will be billed to your Comcast account!

If you have any problems let me know . Future apps can be added, updated or replaced through the Filelinked app.

Toggle Mouse

★★★★★

This is a utility app that will give you a mouse function on your firestick remote.

A few apps require a mouse. This will enable you to navigate through those apps.

To activate and use:

1. double click the Play/Pause button

2. navigate with the navigation circle: up/ down/right/left.

3. To make mouse circle invisible: click the select button, then back button.

4. Click Play/Pause once and hold to deactivate.

Trakt TV

★★★★★

A free service that monitors and tags all the movies and TV shows you view with your firestick so that you always know what you watched and where you leave off on a TV series. Sign up for a free account here then in your CyberFlix settings register with Trakt by entering the code provided and thats it. Trakt will do the rest.
