Rev Owens is a caring, compassionate Shepherd and anointed preacher. He has recently come to the Brick Church where, through the power of the Spirit, and to the glory of God, he has been an inspiration. He has a heart for the hurting and a desire to bring people closer to the Lord. The messages he receives from the God are unique and thought provoking. He teaches the Bible in such a way that makes it practical and relevant to every day life. Rev Owens has authored several books. The Lord gives him insights into truths that are often overlooked. He is anxious to welcome and to serve all who desire to love the Lord.
